The History of Cafe Tato

Since 1968

A Legacy of Taste

Cafe Tato began as a small local eatery serving authentic Maharashtrian snacks and beverages. Over the decades, it became a beloved destination known for its warm atmosphere and timeless flavors.

Growing With Time

Tradition Meets Modern Comfort

While preserving its original recipes and heritage, Cafe Tato evolved into a modern dining space where generations come together to enjoy memorable meals and conversations.

About Us

Serving Tradition Since 1968

Cafe Tato is more than just a restaurant — it is a part of Goa’s food culture. Known for its authentic Goan flavors, warm hospitality, and timeless recipes, Cafe Tato has been loved across generations.

From our famous pav bhaji and batata vada to comforting filter coffee and traditional snacks, every dish is prepared with care, consistency, and heritage at its heart.
